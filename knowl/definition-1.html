I'm Xiang Li 李想, a first year math PhD student of algebra and number theory in the University of Edinburgh working with Prof. Minhyong Kim. My interests are in algebraic number theory and arithmetic geometry. To be more specific, I am interested in non-abelian Chabauty methods, which provide an efficient way to find rational points on a curve. I'm also interested in some mathematical physics such as topological quantum field theory and higher category.
