Welcome to my personal website! Here is the definition of me.

Definition 1.1.1.
I'm Xiang Li 李想, a first year PhD student of algebra and number theory in the University of Edinburgh working with Prof. Minhyong Kim. I am a member of Hodge Institute. My interest lies in arithmetic geometry. To be more specific, I am interested in non-abelian chabauty methods, which provide an efficient way to find rational/integral points on a curve. I'm also interested in some mathematical physics like TQFT and higher category.
